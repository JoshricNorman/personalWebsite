The Office of Environmental Justice Affairs is an office under the Associated Students of the University of California, San Diego. As an employee of the Associated Students Graphic Studio (ASGS) I was commisioned to create a new logo for them. However, rather than stick to a single logo, the president of this office decided to keep both iterations of my designs. She made the executive decision to use one as the more prominent face of Environmental Justice Affairs while the latter would be a secondary logo
