Whatever it is, rest assured I'm seeking to improve and demonstrate my skills! You can count on me!

Why Graphic Design?

While the old addage is 'Don't judge a book by its cover', there's a certain professionalism that comes with a thoughtfully designed graphic. I firmly believe that a cohesive visual aesthetic is paramount to any project being taken seriously.

With that in mind, I got into Graphic Design simply because I didn't believe I had the capacity to draw or paint well at a younger age. With vector-based graphics, I was able to bring to life my imagination in a much more forgiving media. This eventually led to my love for Graphic Design.
