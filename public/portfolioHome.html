Bear Garden 2015-16

Bear Garden is a recurring event on the University of California San Diego's campus. With each iteration comes a new theme. As a result, the artist in charge must create something cute and appealing while also keeping in line with certain guidelines. Graphics requested include posters, signage, social media images, and banners to name a few.
